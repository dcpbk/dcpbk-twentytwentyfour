Get more involved with DCPBK!

Our board is always looking to engage passionate, service-minded members looking to help our association get to the next level. Whether your interests lie in running programs, event planning, fundraising, networking, or serving on a committee, we have opportunities for everyone to contribute in meaningful ways. Gain valuable workplace and leadership skills while building strong friendships with like-minded individuals in your community. Serving DCPBK is an experience unlike any other -- see for yourself!

To apply for any of the following vacancies, please send your resume and a brief statement of interest to volunteer@dcpbk.org. Volunteers must be members of DCPBK in good standing.
